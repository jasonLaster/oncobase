# About this wiki

This is a personal medical knowledge base for managing a family
member's breast cancer treatment. It collects research, appointment
notes, and decisions in one place that's easy to search and easy to
hand off.

## How it's organized

- **`wiki/`** — synthesized notes: treatment plans, summaries, decision logs.
- **`sources/`** — raw materials: appointment recordings, papers, lab reports.
- **`about/`** — meta: this page, glossary, change log.

## Conventions

- Dates in filenames use `MM-DD` for the current year, `YYYY-MM-DD`
  when the year matters.
- Frontmatter `tags` are lowercase, hyphenated. Skim the
  [tag list](/tags) to see what's already in use.
- Patient-identifying details belong only in the local Obsidian vault;
  the published site stores redacted content.
